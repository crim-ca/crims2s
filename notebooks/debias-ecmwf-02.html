Unbiased ECMWF¶

Here we propose a small model which is a debiased ECMWF forecast according to the data we have. The plan is

  • Compute the bias between the ECMWF model and the observations
  • Make a debiased model
  • Turn this model into a probabilistic forecast
  • Score the forecast For now we do it only on temperature and weeks 3-4.
